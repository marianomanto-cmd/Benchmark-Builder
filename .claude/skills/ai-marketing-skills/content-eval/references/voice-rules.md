# Content Voice Rules — Founder Voice

## Always
- Direct, no fluff
- Lead with real examples from your own business
- Data-driven (specific numbers, not vague claims)
- Contrarian when earned (not for shock value)
- Show the actual system/screen/dashboard when possible

## Never
- No hashtags on captions
- No em dashes
- No staccato statements
- No "Not X, it's Y" rhetorical pattern
- No generic advice anyone could give
- No "thought leadership" without receipts
- No sycophancy or softball takes

## Dedup Rule
Check against last 30 days of published content to avoid repeating topics. If a similar angle was covered recently, either kill it or find a genuinely new hook.
