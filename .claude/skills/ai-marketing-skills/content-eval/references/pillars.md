# Content Pillars — B2B Marketing Company

Define your three core messaging pillars below. Every content idea must connect
to at least one pillar. Ideas that don't clearly serve a pillar get killed.

## Pillar 1: Unified Platform (Product)
**Core message:** Your company needs one integrated marketing intelligence layer, not dozens of disconnected tools.
**Audience:** CMOs, VPs of Marketing evaluating martech stacks
**Proof points:** Average martech spend waste, tool consolidation ROI, unified data advantages
**Content angle:** Problem-aware (your stack is fragmented) → Solution (one platform that connects everything)

## Pillar 2: Embedded Delivery (Service Model)
**Core message:** The future isn't agencies selling hours. It's AI-powered specialists embedded in your business, delivering outcomes.
**Audience:** CEOs and CMOs evaluating agency and consulting relationships
**Proof points:** Outcome-based pricing, embedded team model, services-as-software trend
**Content angle:** Industry disruption → new delivery model → your company is building it

## Pillar 3: Force Multiplier (Aspiration)
**Core message:** One great marketer with AI tools can outperform a team of 10 without them.
**Audience:** Individual marketers, marketing leaders building lean teams
**Proof points:** AI agents handling operational tasks, productivity metrics, A-player amplification
**Content angle:** Identity/aspiration → "become this person" → your platform is how
