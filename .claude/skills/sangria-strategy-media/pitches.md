# Client pitches, proposals & credentials

## Pitch types
- **Agency RFP response** (e.g. the Copa Digital Media RFP) - the full "are we the right partner?" response.
- **Credentials / GTM** (e.g. `Sangria_Credentials_2026_GTM`) - agency story, methodology, services, proof.
- **Client proposal / scope** - an engagement or campaign proposal; performance partnership, project, or creative-development SOW; usually carries the client's campaign code.
- **Capabilities / partner pitch** (e.g. a Biddeo.io capabilities pitch).
- **Creator-deal pitch** (e.g. Copa YouTube Creator Deals).
- **Activation proposal** (e.g. "The Copa Hour").

## The two agency frames
- **"Clients Deserve Better"** - "we exist because we believe clients deserve better partnerships, ideas, expertise, results." A two-decade principle; ask "what else? how can we be better?" every day. Best for relationship/incumbent pitches.
- **"Above the Algo / Making people feel brands"** - idea-led experiences designed for the algorithmic age, delivered at global scale; enhanced by AI, culturally relevant, data-enabled. Best for brand-led pitches.
Both share a spine: strategy-first, backed by media + creativity; data-first; work that wins in the real world.

## The agency-RFP deck arc
1. **Opening letter** - signed, personal. Reframe the RFP's real question ("Is the client partnered with the right team to drive future success?") and answer it with shared history, trust, and proof. Name the client's brand equity and mission. Close warm ("Let's Fly").
2. **Meet [Agency]** - who we are; beliefs; "Strategy First - backed by Media & Creativity"; expertise pillars (Data Intelligence, Integrated Strategy, Media & Influence, Creative Experiences, Performance Analysis); the **Brilliant Basics**; **Tier-1 partner access** (Google/Meta/TikTok/Microsoft/Pinterest + travel publishers); a bespoke asset (e.g. **CopaGPT**, a secure client-specific marketing AI/knowledge base); mission ("Work that wins in the real world"); origin story; values.
3. **Experience & Case Studies** - proof (format below).
4. **The Assignment** - the actual strategy for the brief at hand (e.g. Panama Stopover) - pull from `media-strategy.md` and `creative-concept-and-comms.md`.
5. **Analytics / Dashboards** and **Attribution** - capability sections (detail lives in sangria-agency-os; keep conceptual here).
6. **Why us / "We're Great Travel Partners"** - the partnership argument (below).

## Sangria's pitch philosophy
Ground every target in evidence (research partners like Mintel/Kantar; prior results; category structure). Be honest even when it costs the easy close. Calibrate expectations explicitly. Lead with the relationship when you have one. Be brilliant at the basics before promising big ideas. Stake part of compensation on the outcome where credible.

### The proposal arc (for proposals/SOWs)
Why we built it this way -> the baseline/evidence -> measurement calendar -> strategic approach (territory + how we win) -> fee structure + triggers (as structure) -> scenario summary (downside/base/target) -> important clarifications -> our position.

## Case-study format
Bold title (e.g. "Make It Happen", "Enjoy the Journey", "Panama Stopover Launch", "Butts in Seats") + **Challenge -> Overview -> Strategy -> Results** (1-2 hero metrics). Often anchored by a one-word emotional theme. Strategic plays to name: **conquesting** (seize a competitor's repositioning - e.g. Colombia vs Avianca going low-cost), **lifecycle / value-not-volume** (e.g. Hertz car rentals), **playbook-for-many-markets** (e.g. Visit Florida / Carl's Jr social playbook across 45+ markets).

## "Work that wins in the real world" (creative filter)
Category Distinctive (grounded in undeniable truths, a clear edge) - Culturally Disruptive (become part of culture, don't compete with it) - Consumer Persuasive (right message, right audience, right platform, right moment).

## The partnership argument ("Agency = Agent")
- **Fiduciary & transparent** - no hidden fees, kickbacks, MFA sites, bots, or non-transparent buying; every dollar visible; act in the client's best interest.
- **Selfless when it matters** - prioritize partnership over profit (e.g. during COVID); always-on through crises and sensitive moments.
- **A "yes, we can do it" mindset** - within scope or beyond it.
- **Unique offering**: **"Freedom within a Framework"** (checks & balances, shared accountability), **depth of specialist talent & Tier-1 partners**, and **"AOR on Demand" (not traditional AOR)** - the right talent for the right task at the right time, no change orders, "move at the speed of finance", with a single always-on integrated client lead.
- Aligned, predictable, transparent fees; commitment even without a formal MSA.
