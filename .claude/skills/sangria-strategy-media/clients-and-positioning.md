# Clients & positioning (conceptual specifics)

## Copa Airlines - campaign/IO, plus co-funded partners (the anchor relationship)
Incumbent partner; pitch from the relationship. Commercial-signal-led (routes, fares, load factor). Recurring **Positioning** campaigns per market (US, Colombia, DR, Puerto Rico, Panama). Brand platform **"Be More."**
- **Stopover** - flagship program; reposition a layover into a reason to fly ("gateway to Latin America"; idle transit -> discovery; free stop up to 7 days in Panama). Persona: **The Curious Explorer**. ~70% of passengers are unaware -> close the awareness gap. Co-funded by **Promtur** (Panama tourism authority) + Copa + ConnectMiles. Landing: stopoverenpanama / -inpanama / -nopanama (ES/EN/PT). Tentpole: **Stopover x World Cup 2026**; 2026 expansion focus on **Brazil**.
- **Loyalty / co-brand** - **ConnectMiles (CMI)** credit card; platform "Connect to what matters most / Earn that feeling"; message tailored by loyalty tier (don't sell status to those who have it).
- **Products & partnerships** - Buy Miles; **ViX**/streaming; tentpoles (World Cup); **destination pushes** (San Diego, Los Cabos) often co-funded with tourism authorities and payment partners (Marqeta, PayPal, Promtur) and run **under Copa's campaign codes**.
- **Conquesting** - e.g. Colombia, seizing the opening when Avianca repositioned low-cost, with a full-service message (onboard service, connectivity, punctuality - "most punctual in Latin America").
- **B2B creation** - LinkedIn content strategy (CMS benchmarking, whitespace, business-traveler targeting).
- Pillars: connectivity, punctuality, onboard experience/comfort, business class, plus destination-fare angles.

## Felix - fintech, AOR
Brand-equity-led. Typology **"Outsider"** -> **Share of Voice > Market Share** + distinctive, high-quality creative. Own the open territory **"Convenience"** (and **Trust**). Remittance corridors across LatAm. Pitches/strategy grounded in tracking-wave baselines and **outcome-staked**.

## Valuetainment - events/content, PBD ecosystem
Event properties: **The Vault, Business Planning Workshop, Sales Leadership Summit**. Conversion = **tickets/registrations**. **Dual-pillar** (Patrick Bet-David personality vs product/workbook). Guiding line: **"educate about Pat first, the event second."** RevOps angle (capture -> nurture -> convert) with creators/ambassadors as a tiered distribution layer.

## Carl's Jr (CKE) - global social content
Bold, crave-worthy brand voice unified across **~38-45 markets** with local flexibility. A **universal content system** (steady appetite-inducing assets) + a **social playbook** (tone, imagery, content mix, engagement) so franchisees activate consistently yet locally; influencer activations amplify. Theme: global consistency + local authenticity ("Cravings", "Belonging"). Also appears as a creator-marketing reference for other clients.

## Crimtan - adtech partner (not a pitch client)
Programmatic/data partner inside demand-creation and retargeting media (open-web reach, audience enrichment, DCO for prospecting + retargeting, dynamic fare insertion, creative rules - e.g. within Copa Stopover). A **capability/partner**, not a client with its own pitches.

## Case-study / reference clients (travel & tourism heritage)
Roughly two decades partnering 20+ leading travel organizations. Named references/cases include **American Express**, **Hertz** (lifecycle / value-not-volume car-rental play), and **Visit Florida** (playbook + 122 campaigns across 45+ markets). Use these as proof of category depth in pitches; they are heritage/reference, distinct from current active clients.

## Cross-client rule
Lead with the relationship where you have one; pick the territory; ground it in evidence; give every channel a role; sequence the message; and write in Sangria's honest, fiduciary voice.
